📘 How to Use This Weather App

🔎 Search a City

If you can't find the search option,click on the three line list bar

Just type the name of a city in the search box and press Enter or click the search icon.

You will see the weather for that city below instantly!

🌦️ What Info You Get

  • Temperature and feels like
  • Humidity and pressure
  • Wind speed and direction
  • City, region, country, time and date
  • Current weather condition

🏙️ Default Cities

The app also shows weather for 4 cities by default:

  • Mumbai
  • Kolkata
  • Delhi
  • Chennai

💡 Tips

  • Make sure you're connected to the internet
  • Type correct city names for best results
  • Refresh the page if data doesn't show up
